Art Department

When you think about how your game should look and feel, the details matter!

Gather the artists in your life to join the art department! A theme, color scheme, logo, props and art on challenges will bring the immersive game experience to the next level. Your team can really add to the vibrancy of a game!

Not really an artist but would love to add some artistic flair to the game? We do have some talented folks within our community that can be hired and commissioned to make props. Talk with other game hosts for recommendations!
