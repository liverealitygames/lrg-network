Guided Questions & Quick Thoughts

Photo: Survifern

1. What kind of format do you want to host?

  • Based on an existing reality TV show, such as Survivor, Big Brother, The Challenge, The Mole, The Traitors etc?
  • Similar to an existing reality TV show, but with notable differences?
  • A new original format?

2. Where do you want to host the game?

  • Geographically, where?
  • Inside a house or outdoors?
  • If outdoors, do you have a piece of land secured such as a campground or a local park?
  • Does the location have a place for volunteers or eliminated players to stay?

Photo: Outlast

3. How long do you want to run the game?

  • There are many 1-day and weekend games out there!
  • Games go up to 7-8 days at max. Longer games may struggle casting players and/or higher expenses.

4. How do you want the game to be seen, if at all?

  • Do you plan to promote the game anywhere?
  • Do you plan to live stream the game through Facebook or YouTube?
  • Do you plan to film and create episodes?

5. What is the cost of the game?

  • What is your cost to run the game?
  • What is the cost you will charge the players?
  • Is there a prize associated with the game? Is the prize a memento or cash?

6. Do you have a team of volunteers in place? Who will help you produce the game?

  • Will you ask production to help contribute financially?
  • Many games build production teams over time from past players.

7. How do you plan to do casting?

  • Will you post open applications?
  • How much recruiting will you do?
  • What will you require people to submit (i.e., photos, audition video, etc)?
  • Will you do interviews?

Photo: Survivor Lake of Bays

8. Leading up to the game, how will you have players prepare for the game?

  • Will they need to bring or buy anything?
  • Do you need to coordinate logistics with any traveling players?
  • Will you provide rules or a handbook to players beforehand? Will you have agreements around player conduct, respect for each other, and what they can/can't bring into the game?

9. What kind of hospitality are you providing to players and volunteers?

  • Will you be providing meals? What considerations are also there?
  • Will you provide any sort of transportation? Not just thinking about airfare, but pickups from airports, train stations, etc.

Photo: Survivor Drinking Games

10. What kind of challenges would you want to use in your game?

  • Do you have a way to help players with types of disabilities?

11. If your game involves eliminating players during the game, what will those players do while the game continues?

  • Will you have a way for players to battle back into the game?
  • Will eliminated players be invited to help production and/or be involved in the game in another way?
  • Will eliminated players stay on site or leave?

12. How do you plan to help players debrief from the game?
